Why It Matters

Autonomous trading needs safety benchmarks, not just alpha claims.

Trading agents can create harmful patterns such as bursty order flow, runaway inventory, spoof-like behavior, or volatility feedback. MarketImmune makes those behaviors visible, measurable, and explainable in a controlled environment.
